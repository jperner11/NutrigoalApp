# Client Tester Guide

## What to test first
1. Open the invite from your Personal Trainer.
2. Accept the trainer connection.
3. Confirm you can see that you are connected to your trainer.
4. Open your diet and training tabs.
5. Confirm assigned plans appear once your trainer publishes them.
6. Log a meal and complete a workout if assigned.
7. Send a message to your trainer.
8. Complete any feedback request sent to you.

## What “good” should feel like
- You should know who your trainer is.
- You should know whether plans are assigned yet.
- You should not feel pushed into building your own plans if you are trainer-managed.
- Messaging and plan-following should feel simple and clear.

## Report if you notice
- Unclear onboarding
- Missing trainer connection
- Missing plans
- Broken logs or tracking
- Confusing screens or wording
