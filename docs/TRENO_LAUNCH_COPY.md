## 6. About / "What is Treno" Page

# What is Treno

**Treno is a training app. One word. One job.**

Diet, lifting, water, supplements, progress photos, AI-built plans. And — the part nobody else does — your coach lives inside it too.

### Why one app?
Because the current stack is a mess. Macros in one app. Workouts in another. Photos in a third. Coach in WhatsApp. Treno collapses it. One login, one dataset, one place the work happens.

### Why the coach inside?
Most fitness apps try to replace the coach with AI. We think that's the wrong end of the stick. The coaches doing real work — independent PTs and nutritionists — are stuck in WhatsApp, Trainerize, and shared spreadsheets. Their client experience is fragmented. Their retention suffers. Treno gives them programmes, check-ins, video form reviews, and a marketplace listing — all native.

### What's the AI for?
The days you train alone. AI-built diet plans tuned to your goal, body, and training load. AI-built training programmes with RIR, deload weeks, progressive overload. Refeed and diet break logic — built in, not bolted on. When you want a real coach, you upgrade to a human. AI is the feature. The coach is the wedge.

### Who is this for?
People who train. Lifters, runners, hybrid athletes. Anyone who's tired of paying for four apps and getting half-answers from each. And the independent PTs and nutritionists who want to run a real business without duct tape.

### Why "Treno"?
*Treno* means "training" in Portuguese. The founder is Brazilian, based in the UK. One word, one job.

### What's on the roadmap?
- Custom anamnesis flows for coaches
- Free-text food logging (no more searching a database)
- Exercise video library with form references
- Supplement and cardio plan builders
- Deeper AI coaching hub — RIR adjustments, refeed scheduling, deload triggers

We ship weekly. Build notes go out in-app and by email.

One app. Your coach included.
