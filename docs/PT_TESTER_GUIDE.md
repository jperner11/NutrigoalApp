# Personal Trainer Tester Guide

## What to test first
1. Create or sign in to your Personal Trainer account.
2. Open the client roster.
3. Invite a client by email.
4. Confirm the invite appears as pending.
5. After the client accepts, confirm they move into active clients.
6. Assign a diet plan and a training plan.
7. Send a message.
8. Send a feedback request.
9. Confirm you can see client progress and status from your dashboard.

## What “good” should feel like
- You should never feel like you are in the same dashboard as a normal user.
- Invite status should be obvious.
- It should be clear which clients need action.
- Client assignment and communication should feel operational, not improvised.

## Report if you notice
- Any point where you are unsure what to do next
- Invite confusion or acceptance issues
- Clients missing from roster
- Plans not appearing for clients
- Messaging or feedback inconsistencies
