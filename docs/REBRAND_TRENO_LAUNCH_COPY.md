## 6. "What Changed" Page

# What changed

**Short answer: the name. That's it.**

### Why did Meal & Motion become Treno?
The old name described two features. Treno describes the job — training. *Treno* means "training" in Portuguese. The founder is Brazilian, based in the UK. One word, one focus, room to grow into the coach marketplace we're building.

### Do I need to do anything? Will I lose my data or login?
No. Same login, same password, same data. Your macros, photos, programmes, history, streaks — all of it. Open the app, it just says Treno now. If something looks wrong, email founder@treno.co.uk. It comes to a human.

### Did pricing change?
No. Same plans, same prices. Free, Pro, and Unlimited all stay where they were. If you're a PT Client, your coach still pays. If you're a Personal Trainer, your dashboard is in the same place — just rebranded.

### What's this about coaches in the app?
Treno includes a coach marketplace. Independent PTs and nutritionists run their entire client business inside Treno — anamnesis, programmes, check-ins, video form reviews, supplement and cardio plans. If you have a coach, invite them. If you want one, the marketplace is rolling out now.

### What's on the roadmap?
- Custom anamnesis flows for coaches
- Free-text food logging (no more searching a database)
- Exercise video library with form references
- Supplement and cardio plan builders
- Deeper AI coaching hub — RIR adjustments, refeed scheduling, deload triggers

We ship weekly. Build notes go out in-app and by email.

One app. Your coach included.
