# Meal & Motion — Beta Tester Guide

Welcome — and thank you for spending real time on this. You're one of four people testing Meal & Motion before it opens up. Your honest reactions are the entire point.

This isn't a polished launch — expect rough edges. **Tell us about them.** That's the job.

---

## What we're testing

There are two ways to use Meal & Motion. You'll have access to one of them depending on who invited you:

- **As an individual** — you're using it to plan your own meals and training, with optional coach support
- **As a coach** — you're using it to run your clients (delivery, messaging, check-ins, marketplace profile)

If you don't know which one you are, ask.

---

## Getting started

### If you're an individual

1. Go to **https://[YOUR-DOMAIN]** and click **Start free**
2. Pick **"Myself"**, create your account
3. Complete onboarding — be honest about goals, schedule, food preferences. The AI uses everything you tell it
4. Once onboarding finishes, your dashboard loads. From there:
   - Try generating a meal plan
   - Try generating a training plan
   - Log a meal, log water, log a workout
   - Browse `/find-coach` and look at the two test coaches
   - If you have a coach invite link, accept it and explore the coached side

### If you're a coach

1. Go to **https://[YOUR-DOMAIN]** and click **Start free**
2. Pick **"My clients"**, create your account
3. Complete the coach onboarding — public profile, ideal clients, coaching style, intake setup
4. From your dashboard, try:
   - Inviting a client by email (a real address you control)
   - Reviewing what a lead looks like in the inbox
   - Building a meal plan and a training plan from scratch
   - Assigning them to a (test) client
   - Sending a message
   - Reviewing a check-in (if any have been submitted)

## What to pay attention to

These are the questions we care about. If you have an answer to any of them by the end of week 1, that's gold:

1. **Where did you get stuck?** Anywhere you had to guess what to do next, or gave up.
2. **What was unclear?** Wording, button labels, what data goes where.
3. **What broke?** Anything that 500'd, redirected weirdly, lost your input, or showed wrong data.
4. **What did you actually use?** What you opened day-to-day vs. what you opened once and never returned to.
5. **What's missing?** What did you expect to be there that wasn't?
6. **Would you pay for this?** And if so, what feels fair?

You don't have to answer everything. One blunt sentence per finding is more useful than a polite paragraph.

## Known rough edges (no need to report)

- **Visual inconsistency** — some pages still look "older SaaS," others have the new editorial style. We're mid-rebrand. Tell us if it's actively confusing, but don't bother flagging mismatched buttons or fonts.
- **Mobile app** — there is one, but it's not the focus of this beta. Stick to web.
- **Stripe billing** — disabled for beta. You're on Unlimited regardless of what the pricing page says.
- **Marketplace** — only two test coaches are seeded. The directory will look sparse.
